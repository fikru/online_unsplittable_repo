long rnd32 ( long  seed  ) 

Congruential generator of pseudorandom sequences of numbers uniformly distributed between 1 and 2147483646, using the congruential relation: Xn+1 = 16807 * Xn mod 2147483647 .

The input to the routine is the integer Xn, while the returned integer is the number Xn+1.
